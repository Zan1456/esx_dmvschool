General theory test


Online test for the theoretical driver's license test! Right of way rules, traffic signs, behavior in the event of danger - in our driver's license test you will receive a questionnaire with test questions compiled at random - just like in the official test.


For each question you can choose from predefined answers. The evaluation shows whether you would have passed and are still fit for road traffic. The entire theoretical test takes about 5 to 10 minutes, there is no maximum duration. A total of 10 questions must be answered.


Depending on the level of difficulty of the questions, there are different numbers of error points. The most difficult category has four demerits. The theoretical driving test is passed if fewer than fifteen error points have been achieved at the end.
